## Where to Connect

To connect, place a cross-connection order with the meet-me room (MMR) operator at your chosen facility. Cross-connect pricing is set by the MMR operator. *SFMIX does not pay for participant-facing cross-connects.*

All locations offer **1, 10, 100, or 400Gbps** fiber connectivity.

*SMF only (10km 1310nm optics). No MMF or copper. Tunable or colored DWDM optics required in the Astound suite (passive MUX to SFMIX). BiDi available for 10G and 100G. New 1G ports use a rate-limited 10G PHY.*
