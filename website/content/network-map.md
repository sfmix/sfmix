## The SFMIX fabric, live

A subway-style map of the SFMIX backbone across the Bay Area. Stations are our
datacenters; the coloured lines are the dark-fiber circuits between them, shaded
by how busy each link is right now. Zoom out for a clean metro-level overview;
zoom in to a site to see its individual switches. Click any link or station for
details. Routes are drawn approximately — they follow the real corridors, not
exact fiber paths.
