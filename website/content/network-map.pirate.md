## Th' SFMIX waters, live an' churnin'

A chart o' the SFMIX backbone, drawn like a landlubber's subway map o'er the whole
Bay. The ports be our datacenters; the coloured lines be the dark-fiber runs betwixt
'em, shaded by how heavy each be laden right now. Pull back yer spyglass for a tidy
harbour-by-harbour view; sail in close to a berth to spy its lone switches. Give any
line or port a click for the full manifest. The routes be drawn by dead reckonin' —
they hug the true channels, not the exact rope o' fiber. Here be packets.
