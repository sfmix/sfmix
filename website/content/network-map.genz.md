## The SFMIX fabric, live (no cap)

It's giving subway map, but for the SFMIX backbone across the Bay. Stations = our
datacenters, the colored lines = the dark-fiber links between them, shaded by how
cooked each link is rn. Zoom out for a clean metro-level view; zoom into a site to
see its actual switches. Tap any link or station for the details. Heads up: the
routes are approximate — they follow the real corridors, not the exact fiber path.
It's lowkey the move.
