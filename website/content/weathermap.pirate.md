Th' classic weather chart o' the backbone: every switch in the SFMIX fleet an'
every rope o' fiber betwixt 'em, coloured by how heavy laden each one be right
now. Every parallel strand be drawn as its own line, split amidships so ye can
spy both directions on their own — the arrows point where the bits be sailin'.
Hover yer spyglass or give any line a tap for the full manifest.
