SFMIX network map — loader lab

Loading-screen concepts for the network map. Every demo runs over the real live-loading map (the private demo build, iframed underneath) and dismisses on the map's genuine first idle — so what you feel here is real load timing, not a canned delay. The HUD in the corner shows when the map was actually ready vs. when the loader revealed it. Reload a page to replay (second loads are faster: warm cache).
